This is a zoomable Sunburst visualization, depicting a breakdown of passengers on the Titanic, divided by their class, gender, and if they survived or died in the shipwreck

To interact with the graph, click on one of the sections.
By hovering over a section, you can see the size and percentage of the total.

To zoom, click on a section of the graph. The new relative percentages will show, as well as the percentage of the total.
